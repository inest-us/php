The font-size property supports a variety of methods for specifying a font size. For example, there are seven different absolute size keywords, which set the font size relative to the user's font size preference.

  • xx-small
  • x-small
  • small
  • medium
  • large
  • x-large
  • xx-large

You can also make fonts larger or smaller by way of the larger or smaller keywords.

You can make fonts 50% larger or 25% smaller by way of percentages.

You can even make a font 50% larger or 25% smaller by way of em units.
